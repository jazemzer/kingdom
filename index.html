    Kingdom of God - Study Overview

    The purpose of this study is to

    • find out all about Kingdom of God
    • prepare a checklist that anyone can use for self-introspection
    • find practical ways to qualify

    Why is it important to study about the Kingdom of God

    The simple answer is we are asked to do so - But seek ye first the kingdom of God, and his righteousness; and all these things shall be added unto you Mt 6: 33.

    But a nice side benefit is that we have all our needs covered. As 'all things' in the above verse refers to the worries of life and bodyMt 6: 25. Specifically, what to eat, what to drink, and what to wear Mt 6: 31.
